KIRSTEN GRACE N. JABINAL

Greetings, everyone! Today is Sunday and I'm on my way to visit my grandparents. As I am riding the vehicle,
let me give you an introduction about myself. I am 17 years old, I was born on the 4th of September year 2007
in Quezon City. I am currently a 1st Year, BS-Psychology student at New Era University. I was a former
Information and Communication Technology student, Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track. What a twist, right?
Lol. Every Sunday we gather around at my grandparents' house. We hold different kinds of activities such as
singing or what we call videoke, the boys playing basketball, playing badminton, playing golf, or even riding a
bike. Sometimes we go to the mall. We usually eat our lunch there and visit an arcade. The activities I
mentioned are also my hobbies, including binge-watching. For dislikes, I don't want foods or drinks that are not tasty.

Now let's go back to myself. As you can see, my family are loving and caring, but that doesn't save me from being
insecure about my looks, overthinking everything, and doubting myself. I don't think I am good enough to be able
to live by myself. I wanna stay living a disney princess life, receiving a princess treatment from my family makes
me feel like I'm living the life and not just existing. However, small things bothers me. One minor inconvenience
could make me sob. It could ruin everything. So, now I'm on the process of healing. I want everyone to know that
you are not alone. Whatever happens, happens. Keep fighting and aim to live. We only live once. Let's enjoy this
life. Do whatever makes you happy. Be kind. Come and be with me through this journey!
